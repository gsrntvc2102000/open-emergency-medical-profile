Insulin Dependent | Severe Hypoglycemia

Severe Hypoglycemia Alert

ER Instructions:

Check the patient's personal bag for necessary medical supplies (e.g., medications, monitoring devices).

If the patient shows signs of confusion, dizziness, sudden falling, or loss of consciousness, follow these steps:

  • Check for medical alert bracelets, ID cards, or digital emergency profiles.
  • Look for emergency medications or treatments (e.g., glucose tablets, epinephrine, inhalers).
  • If low blood sugar or a medical emergency is suspected, provide a quick source of sugar:
    • Glucose tablets or gels
    • Sweet juice (orange, apple, etc.)
    • Hard candy or sugar water
  • Call emergency services if the patient is unresponsive.

Emergency action should be taken immediately if symptoms worsen.
